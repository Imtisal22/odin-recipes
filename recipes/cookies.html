Cookies

Description

This is the perfect cookie recipe! It's chewy, fudgy and moist.

Ingredients

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg + 1 egg yolk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Whisk the flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt together in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk the melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until no brown sugar lumps remain.
  3. Whisk in the egg and egg yolk.
  4. Finally, whisk in the vanilla extract.
  5. The mixture will be thin.
  6. Pour into dry ingredients and mix together with a large spoon or rubber spatula.
  7. The dough will be very soft, thick, and appear greasy.
  8. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  9. The chocolate chips may not stick to the dough because of the melted butter, but do your best to combine them.
  10. Cover the dough tightly and chill in the refrigerator for at least 2 to 3 hours or up to 3 days.
  11. Take the dough out of the refrigerator and allow it to slightly soften at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  12. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C). Line large baking sheets with parchment paper or silicone baking mats. Set aside.
  13. Using a scoop heap of dough. Roll into a ball,repeat with the remaining dough and places the ball on the cookie sheet.
  14. Bake the cookies for 12 to 13 minutes or until the edges are very lightly browned.
